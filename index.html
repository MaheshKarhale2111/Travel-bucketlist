Places I want to visit in 2023...

Kerala

Known as ‘God’s Own Country’, picturesque Kerala is a must on any travel bucket list. The more laidback pace of life is the perfect introduction to India and is a stark contrast to bustling destinations such as the Golden Triangle. This wonderful state right at the bottom of India has so much to offer, from tantalising cuisine, wonderful wildlife, mesmerising culture and lots to explore. Getting to Kerala is surprisingly straightforward, with direct flights from London and flights with one in-airport transfer from many major UK cities. If you’re wondering when to visit, December and January can be very busy, and the monsoon season from June until the end of August is best avoided. From the end of September is our favourite time, when the greenery comes to life after the heavy rain.

Pondicherry

Pondicherry, also known as Puducherry, is a union territory in India known for its French colonial history, beautiful beaches, and diverse culture. Some reasons to visit Pondicherry include: Experience French culture: Pondicherry was a French colony until 1954 and still retains a strong French influence in its architecture, food, and way of life. Visitors can visit the French Quarter, which features French-style houses and street names written in French. Relax on the beach: Pondicherry has a number of beautiful beaches, including Promenade Beach, Serenity Beach, and Auroville Beach. These beaches are perfect for swimming, sunbathing, and enjoying the beautiful scenery.

Auroville

Auroville is an experimental international township located near Pondicherry in the union territory of India. It was founded in 1968 by Mirra Alfassa, also known as "The Mother," as a place where people of all nationalities could live in harmony. Some reasons to visit Auroville include: Learn about alternative ways of living: Auroville is an intentional community that is designed to be self-sustaining and environmentally friendly. Visitors can learn about the community's way of life, which emphasizes sustainability, self-sufficiency, and spiritual growth. Visit the Matrimandir: The Matrimandir is a large golden dome that is the spiritual center of Auroville. Visitors can take a tour of the Matrimandir and learn about its significance to the community.

Manali

Manali is a hill station in the state of Himachal Pradesh in northern India. It is a popular tourist destination known for its natural beauty, adventure sports, and cultural attractions. Some reasons to visit Manali include: Enjoy the natural beauty: Manali is surrounded by snow-capped mountains and is home to a number of beautiful waterfalls, such as the Jogini Waterfall and the Vashisht Temple Waterfall. Visitors can also take a tour of the Rohtang Pass, which offers stunning views of the Himalayas. Try adventure sports: Manali is a popular destination for adventure sports such as paragliding, rafting, and rock climbing. There are a number of tour companies that offer guided adventures for visitors who want to try these activities.

Andaman

The Andaman and Nicobar Islands are a group of islands located in the Bay of Bengal in India. They are known for their beautiful beaches, clear blue waters, and rich biodiversity. Some reasons to visit the Andaman and Nicobar Islands include: Enjoy the beaches: The Andaman and Nicobar Islands are home to a number of beautiful beaches, such as Radhanagar Beach, Elephant Beach, and Kalapathar Beach. These beaches are perfect for swimming, sunbathing, and relaxing in the beautiful surroundings. Explore the marine life: The Andaman and Nicobar Islands are home to a rich variety of marine life, including dolphins, sea turtles, and colorful fish. Visitors can go snorkeling or scuba diving to explore the underwater world.

Kashmir

Kashmir is a region in northern India known for its natural beauty, including mountains, lakes, and gardens. Some reasons to visit Kashmir include: Enjoy the natural beauty: Kashmir is home to a number of beautiful natural attractions, such as the Dal Lake, the Mughal Gardens, and the Gulmarg ski resort. Visitors can take a houseboat ride on the Dal Lake, go skiing at Gulmarg, or enjoy the beautiful gardens. Visit cultural attractions: Kashmir is home to a number of cultural attractions, including temples, mosques, and palaces. Visitors can visit the Shankaracharya Temple, the Jamia Masjid mosque, and the Maharaja Hari Singh Palace to learn about the region's history and culture. Try adventure sports: Kashmir is a popular destination for adventure sports such as paragliding, rafting, and trekking. There are a number of tour companies that offer guided adventures for visitors who want to try these activities.

Rome

Rome is the capital city of Italy and is known for its rich history, beautiful architecture, and delicious food. Some reasons to visit Rome include: Explore the city's rich history: Rome is home to a number of historical sites and landmarks, including the Colosseum, the Pantheon, and the Trevi Fountain. Visitors can take a tour of these and other historical sites to learn about the city's rich past. Admire the city's beautiful architecture: Rome is known for its beautiful architecture, including ancient Roman buildings, Baroque churches, and Renaissance palaces. Visitors can take a stroll through the city to admire the beautiful buildings and landmarks.

Maldives

The Maldives is a country located in the Indian Ocean that is known for its beautiful beaches, clear blue waters, and luxury resorts. Some reasons to visit the Maldives include: Relax on the beach: The Maldives is home to a number of beautiful beaches, including Bikini Beach and Paradise Island Beach. These beaches are perfect for swimming, sunbathing, and enjoying the beautiful surroundings. Explore the marine life: The Maldives is home to a rich variety of marine life, including dolphins, sharks, and colorful fish. Visitors can go snorkeling or scuba diving to explore the underwater world. Stay in a luxury resort: The Maldives is home to a number of luxury resorts that offer overwater bungalows, spas, and other amenities. These resorts are a great place to relax and unwind in style.

Venice

Venice is a city in northeastern Italy that is known for its canals, beautiful architecture, and rich cultural history. Some reasons to visit Venice include: Explore the city's canals: Venice is famous for its canals, which are a unique feature of the city. Visitors can take a boat ride along the canals to see the city from a different perspective. Admire the city's beautiful architecture: Venice is home to a number of beautiful buildings and landmarks, including the Basilica di San Marco, the Palazzo Ducale, and the Ponte di Rialto. Visitors can take a stroll through the city to admire the beautiful architecture. Visit cultural attractions: Venice is home to a number of cultural attractions, including museums, galleries, and theaters. Visitors can visit the Gallerie dell'Accademia, which houses a collection of paintings by artists such as Bellini and Titian, or the Teatro La Fenice, a famous opera house.

Tokyo

Tokyo is the capital city of Japan and is known for its bustling streets, unique culture, and delicious food. Some reasons to visit Tokyo include: Explore the city's unique culture: Tokyo is a city with a rich and unique culture, and there are many ways to experience it. Visitors can visit temples and shrines, try on traditional Japanese clothing, or participate in a tea ceremony. Visit cultural attractions: Tokyo is home to a number of cultural attractions, including museums, galleries, and theaters. Visitors can visit the Tokyo National Museum, which houses a collection of Japanese art and artifacts, or the Kabuki-za Theatre, which hosts traditional Japanese theater performances.

Bali

Bali is an Indonesian island located in the westernmost end of the Lesser Sunda Islands, lying between Java to the west and Lombok to the east. It is known for its beautiful beaches, lush forests, and unique culture. Some reasons to visit Bali include: Relax on the beach: Bali is home to a number of beautiful beaches, including Kuta Beach, Seminyak Beach, and Nusa Dua Beach. These beaches are perfect for swimming, sunbathing, and enjoying the beautiful surroundings. Explore the island's unique culture: Bali is home to a unique culture that is a blend of traditional Indonesian and Hindu traditions. Visitors can participate in cultural activities such as visiting temples, attending a traditional dance performance, or trying on traditional clothing.
